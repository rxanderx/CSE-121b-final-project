Motivation

I created this project because I wanted to build something that would have use and impact in my life.
I love freestyling and recognized that the thing I need to practice the most is my capability to connect words and topics.
By generating the rhyming words using API calls to an entire thesaurus, I can focus solely on connecting those words and finding connections and wordplay to effectively do so.
